Peterborough Museum & Art Gallery

The exhibits of archaeological, artistic, and social history and culture make a fascinating and educational introduction to the city's opulent heritage.

Opening Times: Tuessday to Saturday, 10:00 - 16:00, last entrance 15:30

Accessibilty: Wheelchair accessible (book the single disabled parking bay ahead). Lift access except Vaults and Surgical Theatre. Largely quiet and uncrowded.

Entrance Fees: Free. Special events may incur a modest fee.



>> Go to Peterborough Museum & Art Gallery's website

Located in Priestgate in the City Centre, Peterborough Museum & Art Gallery takes the visitor on a journey through time, exploring the city's history and culture through the ages.

Fascinating to adults and children alike, it's a must if you have an interest in art and/or history, or if you just want to while away a rainy afternoon.

There is plenty of parking available around teh City Centre, but not on site. Check their website for events and exhibitions.
