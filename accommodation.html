Accommodation in Peterborough

Peterborough offers everything from camping in the serene Nene Valley to the luxury experience of The Bull Hotel. Here we've selected a few of our favourites.

The Bull Hotel

Rooms: Double, Twin, Family, Four Poster Rooms, Suites
Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Weddings, Events, Conference Rooms, Parking, Wi-Fi
Price Range: £££

Located in Westgate, a few minutes walk from the Cathedral, our favourite hotel offers 118 sumptuous rooms and all the facilites you could possibly need for a luxurious stay in Peterborough.

The Pearl Hotel

Rooms: Double, Twin Rooms and Suites
Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Weddings, Events, Conference Rooms, Parking, Wi-Fi
Price Range: ££ - £££

A lovely new boutique hotel in the heart of Peterborough, perfect for leisure and business travellers with spacious, comfortable rooms, very friendly staff and excellent breakfasts.

Travelodge Peterborough Central

Rooms: Double Rooms
Facilities: Parking, Wi-Fi
Price Range: ££

Clean, quiet and comfortable kingsize double rooms, and centrally located just a few minutes walk from Peterborough Cathedral. Offers excellent value for a hotel in the city centre, but if you want much more than a place to lay your head, look elsewhere.
