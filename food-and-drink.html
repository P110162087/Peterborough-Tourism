Peterborough Food & Drink

The range of dining options in Peterborough is colossal, from street eats to fine dining and options to suit every budget; here are some you might enjoy - we certainly do!

Remember that Saturday is the busiest day for restaurants in the centre of town, so if you're planning to visit between 11am and 4pm, it's a good idea to reserve seating in advance, and understand that wait times will be longer than usual.

1498 The Spice Affair

Facilities: Restaurant, Home Delivery (Deliveroo / Just Eat)
Cuisine: Goan Indian
Price Range:, a delicious fusion of Indian and Portuguese flavours, with an enviable reputation as one of the best restaurants that Peterborough has to offer. Licensed to serve alcohol with your meal.

Argo Lounge

Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Cafe
Cuisine: International, Vegetarian & More
Price Range: ££ - £££

Situated next to the Peterborough Cathedral main entrance gates, Argo Lounge offers a stylish dining experience with a well-stocked bar. Be sure to check out their sublime desserts! Some outdoor seating available.

Wildwood

Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Cafe, Cinema, Home Delivery (Deliveroo / Just Eat)
Cuisine: Pizza, Italian, British
Price Range: ££ - £££

Situated just across the sSuare from Peterborough Cathedral, Wildoows treats you to the best pizzas in town and a great range of pasta dishes, made in open kitchens at the back of the restaurant. There's a fantastic bar running down the centre. Very friendly service. Some outdoor seating available.
