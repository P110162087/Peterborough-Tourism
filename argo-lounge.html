Argo Lounge

Argo Lounge offers a stylish dining experience with a well-stocked bar, gluten-free meals, and spectacular desserts.

Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Cafe, Home Delivery (Deliveroo / Just Eat)

Cuisine: International, Vegan/Vegetarian/Pescatarian/Allergy-Friendly Menus

Price Range: ££ - £££

Right next door to Peterborough Cathedral, Argo Lounge couldn't be better located.

A very chic and modern bar and restaurant with wonderfully varied menus, which won't disappoint. If you're on a restrictive diet, whether by choice or necessity, you'll find they cater for you and you won't be disappointed. Be sure to leave roome for dessert!

The bar is as varied as the menus, and they make great cocktails.

Argo Lounge is magic. We’re right in the heart of the town, between our beautiful cathedral the the historic Guildhall, but that’s not why we’re magic. We’ve lovely outdoor space, light and airy interiors, and a fantastic range of menus, including full kids, gluten free and vegan menus; but that’s not why we’re magic. We’re magic because we have a storage room that is longer than the building itself! Spooky!
