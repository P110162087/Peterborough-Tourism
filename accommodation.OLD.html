Peterborough Activities

There's so much to do in Peterborough it's hard to know where to start! A swim at the lido? A spot of axe throwing? How about sky diving? Sports not your thing, then why not try cookery lessons? The options are endless, but we've picked a few things to do here that highlight \peteborough's cultural heritage to get you started.

Wildwood

Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Cafe, Home Delivery (Deliveroo / Just Eat)
Cuisine: Pizza, Italian, British
Price Range: ££ - £££

Situated just across the sSuare from Peterborough Cathedral, Wildoows treats you to the best pizzas in town and a great range of pasta dishes, made in open kitchens at the back of the restaurant. There's a fantastic bar running down the centre. Very friendly service. Some outdoor seating available.
