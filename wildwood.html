Wildwood

Wildwood

Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Cafe, Home Delivery (Deliveroo / Just Eat)
Cuisine: Pizza, Italian, British
Price Range: ££ - £££

>> Go to Wildwood's website

Situated just across the Square from Peterborough Cathedral, Wildoows treats you to the best pizzas in town and a great range of pasta dishes, made in open kitchens at the back of the restaurant.
There's a fantastic bar running down the centre of the restaurant, and they offer a very friendly service.
Some outdoor seating is available with a view of Cathedral Square.

Wildwood even has its own cinema inside! Book a movie and a meal for just £24.95 (Adults) or £9.95 (Kids).

"Our well-balanced menu offers a wide grill selection and provides something for everyone. All produced by using fresh ingredients and serving up delicious food, every time. Including pizza and pasta dishes, as well as fresh salads, burgers, steaks, risottos and indulgent desserts. Little ones love our restaurants and the fun kids’ menu filled with age appropriate activities and healthy food."
-- From Wildwood's Website
