The Pearl Hotel

A luxurious boutique hotel with a superb location in the heart of Peterborough.

Rooms: Double, Twin Rooms and Suites

Facilities: Restaurant, Bar, Weddings, Events, Conference Rooms, Parking, Wi-Fi

Price Range: ££ - £££

Peterborough's newest boutique hotel, The Pearl offers luxury accommodation and friendly, personal attention in a fantastic City Centre location.

Hotel guests can avail themselves of fabulous dining at the adjacent Tavan Restaurant featuring first class Morrocan & Turkish chargrilled cuisine, and tropical refreshment at their cocktail bar.

Parking and wifi are included.

Ideally situated in the heart of Peterborough, this newly opened Hotel is ideal for the corporate or leisure traveller.
