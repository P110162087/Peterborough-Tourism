Peterborough Activities

There's so much to do in Peterborough it's hard to know where to start! Maybe a swim at Peterborough Lido? A spot of axe throwing if you're up for something a little more strenuous? How about skydiving or gliding at Crowland Airfield? If sports are not your thing, then why not try cookery, pottery, or art lessons?

The options are endless, but we've picked a few places to go here that highlight Peteborough's cultural heritage, which should get you started. Have fun!

Flag Fen Archaeology Park

Opening Times: Wednesday to Sunday, 10:00 - 16:00, last entrance 15:15
Accessibilty: Wheelchair accessible with level paths throughout the site, mobility scooters available to borrow, largely quiet and uncrowded.
Entrance Fees: Adults £6, Kids £3, Family £15 (2 adults, up to 3 kids), Carers and under 5s free

A Bronze Age archaeology site with authentic recreations of ancient lifestyles, set in a beatiful landscape. There's a facinating museum on site with locally sourced artefacts, and regular special events.

Peterborough Cathedral

Opening Times: Saturday to Friday 10:00 - 17:00, Sundays 12:00 15:00. Gates to grounds close at 19:30.
Accessibilty: Wheelchair accessible, largely quiet and uncrowded.
Entrance Fees: Free, but donations welcomed. Special events incur modest variable fees.

Peterborough Cathedral is a stunning sight, one of the finest examples of Norman architecture in the UK, and an unmissable highlight of Peterborough's history and cultural heritage. Gothic design, significant Tudor history, and regular events catering for all interests.

Peterborough Museum & Art Gallery

Opening Times: Tuessday to Saturday, 10:00 - 16:00, last entrance 15:30
Accessibilty: Wheelchair accessible (book the single disabled parking bay ahead). Lift access except Vaults and Surgical Theatre. Largely quiet and uncrowded.
Entrance Fees: Free. Special events may incur a modest fee.

An intriguing collection of local finds and fine arts celebrating Peterborough's archaeological, artistic, and social history and culture. Modern displays ensure visitors of all ages are interested, educated and occupied. Regular special events held.
